\section{Experimental Design}
The August target is one licensed domain, one 124M--160M screening model, one primary nonzero lifetime budget, and paired independently seeded chains. Primary proposed outcomes are held-out human negative log-likelihood regret aggregated across the frozen horizon and an independently frozen tail-retention measure. Final test data is excluded from prompts, selection, thresholding, early stopping, and hyperparameter choice. Exact model, data, horizon, budget, and seed values remain to be frozen before primary outcomes.
